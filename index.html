How to Play Wordle Infinite

Basic Rules

The rules are very straightforward. You need to guess a hidden five-letter word within six tries. After each guess, the game provides feedback through colored tiles that indicate how close your guess is to the correct answer.

  • Green: Correct letter in the correct position
  • Yellow: Correct letter but wrong position
  • Gray: Letter not in the word

Step-by-Step Gameplay

Start by entering a valid five-letter word. Once submitted, observe the color hints carefully. Use that feedback to refine your next guess. Continue this process until you either find the correct word or run out of attempts.

Best Tips to Win Every Game

Start with Strong Words

Always begin with a word that contains common vowels and consonants like A, E, R, T, and L. This increases your chances of getting useful hints early.

Avoid Repeating Letters Too Soon

In the first few attempts, try different letters instead of repeating the same ones. This helps you explore more possibilities quickly.

Use Logic, Not Guesswork

Pay attention to color clues and eliminate incorrect options. Logical thinking is the key to solving puzzles efficiently.

Practice Regularly

Since Wordle Infinite allows unlimited gameplay, use that advantage. The more you play, the better your pattern recognition becomes.
